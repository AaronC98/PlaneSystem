8.5.4. Subprogram Renaming Declarations¶

From the point of view of both static and dynamic verification, a renaming-as-body is treated as a one-line subprogram that “calls through” to the renamed unit.

Legality Rules

  1. The aspect_specification on a subprogram_renaming_declaration shall not include any of the SPARK 2014-defined aspects introduced in this document.
